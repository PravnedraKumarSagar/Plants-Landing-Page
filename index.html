About

Without plants there would be no food

Indoor plants reduce components of indoor air pollution, particularly volatile organic compounds (VOC) such as benzene, toluene, and xylene. The compounds are removed primarily by soil microorganisms. Plants can also remove CO2, which is correlated with lower work performance, from indoor areas.

Indoor Plants

Chlorophytum/Spider Plant

This plant is also known as the spider plant. The plant has derived its name from the thin spider leaves. The leaves are light green and spread quite wide, giving a beautiful look to your home.

Areca Palm

A beautiful tropical plant that adds a charm and beachy look to your home. The plant performs quite well in well-drained soil. It does not require much fertilizing and very little water. This is a perennial plant that prefers soil in a moist condition. This is one of the fastest growing plants, which has stunning long and sharp green leaves.

Peace Lily

The peace lily is a stunning indoor plant that has beautiful white flowers. The plant has big and wide green leaves, which look beautiful in indoor settings. The plant requires moist soil and can survive in low watering conditions but not for too long.

Anthurium

Anthurium plants are flowering plants that have huge red flowers which have big, wide green leaves. Choose a well-lit and bright sunlight area for your anthurium plant. The watering of this plant is on a demand basis. These plants love humidity, and thus, they perform quite well in moist soil conditions.

ZZ Plant

ZZ Plant is one of the high tolerant indoor plants which can survive in every condition. It is a tropical perennial plant that has smooth and shiny leaves. These plants perform quite well in low to medium sunlight conditions with a watering frequency of every 2-3 weeks. The plants love humidity and thus, prefer moist soil.

Snake Plant

Snake plants are one of the most highly tolerant plants. They have stunning tall green leaves which grow on their own. The plant would forgive the owner if you missed watering them. Try to plant it in a big pot so that the plants can grow as a bigger plant. These plants are highly adaptable and are well fitted to any condition.
